The Science of Subtext

Hiring decisions aren't just based on what you say, but how you say it. Learn how our proprietary models decode the hidden metrics behind interview success.

Acoustic Resonance Tracking

Words only account for 7% of human communication. We map your vocal pitch, micro-fluctuations, and pacing against successful candidates in your specific target role.

Hesitation & Filler Decay

Long pauses and filler words ("um," "like," "you know") actively decay your perceived confidence. We isolate the exact timestamps where your cognitive load peaked.

Role-Contextual AI

A software engineer in a technical screen is judged differently than a sales director pitching a product. Our algorithms dynamically shift scoring weights to match the role.

Actionable Correction Paths

Identifying the problem is only half the battle. We generate personalized, actionable exercises to recalibrate your speaking patterns before your next interview.

Zero-Trace Privacy Architecture

Your voice and identity are yours. All audio files are processed securely and never stored. The moment your analysis is generated, the raw source files are permanently purged from our memory banks.
